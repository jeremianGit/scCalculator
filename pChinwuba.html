DR. EGO CHINWUBA

LL.B (Hons), B.L, LL.M, Ph.d

Dr Chinwuba is an Associate Professor in the Faculty of Law, University of Lagos. Prior to joining the academia, she had worked at law firm of Aribisala & Co.

Ego is an authority in the area of Property Law where she had presented a lot of scholarly papers.

She is a Consultant to our firm and assists us greatly in areas of research.
