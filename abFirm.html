About Our Firm

Akin Adepoju & Co is a firm of Legal Practitioners with office at Suite 960 Western House, 8/10 Broad Street, Lagos. It is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission with Registration No BN 2201794.

Its areas of practice include the following:

Commercial Practice General Litigation Company Secretarial Property Consultancy Debt Recovery

The firm comprises of 3 legal practitioners with aggregate cognate experience of over 100 years spread amongst the personnel in the firm. Its principal partner, Akin Adepoju was called to the bar in 1985 and had operated as in house corporate legal practitioner for over 25 years. Akin Adepoju had acted in the capacity of Legal Adviser/Company Secretary for 15 years, nine of which were spent in Omegabank Plc and Spring Bank Plc.

In his capacity as Company Secretary/Legal Adviser in Omegabank Plc, he had supervisory responsibility over its Registrars Department and was also involved in corporate finance and capital market activities of the bank. In this respect he was involved in the following among others:

  • 3 Nos recapitalisation exercises in Owena Bank/Omegabank
  • Merger exercise of Omegabank and 5 other banks to form Spring Bank with direct responsibility of supervising the carrying out of due diligence on the respective banks
  • First Ekiti State Bond in 2001
  • Underwriting of National Sports Lottery Plc initial public offer by Spring Bank
  • As a member of the Recapitalisation Committees of Omegabank and later Springbank, worked with others to midwife the recapitalisation exercise and its fallouts.
  • Rendering of legal advisory services for the various corporate finance activities involving customers of the banks where he worked.
